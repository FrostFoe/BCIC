About BCIC College

BCIC College: A Trusted Name for Quality Education. BCIC College is a renowned academic institution operated by Bangladesh Chemical Industries Corporation regulated by the Ministry of Industry, the People’s Republic of Bangladesh. The college lies in the serene and tranquil premises of a 5.16- acre land, contiguous to the National Botanical Garden as well as Dhaka Zoo, from where it is super easy to have access to all advantages of the capital. On the other hand, in the college compound there stands a Shaheed Minar built in an impressively architectural fashion, a striking mural painting remains drawn displaying the themes of the Language Movement and the Liberation War of Bangladesh. Encompassing tall green trees spreading shadows, the playground carpeted with exquisitely trimmed lush green grass, plush sounds like twittering and squeaking of birds parking themselves on the branches of trees, the hovering of golden-winged kites giving shrill cries, the front entry passage between two pleasant flower gardens, and soft, soothing, southern wind ----- with all these the college has turned out to be a visible paradise. This tender touch of nature together with pleasant demeanor of teachers exerts a positive influence on students’ mind that eventually helps them complete their prescribed courses sans stress. The college commenced its journey with the school section in 1983. The Science Group, the Business Studies Group and the Humanities Group of the college section started their glorious journey in 1991, 1996 and 1997 respectively. At present around 2100 students make the college compound vibrant with their lively presence. The necessary number of qualified and experienced teachers and their utmost efforts along with the direct supervision of the college authority have already made a succession of nearly 100% success as well as high rate of GPA-5 holders in the secondary and higher secondary examinations every single year. Spacious classrooms, subject-wise laboratories, a library with the number close to eight thousand books and with good seating arrangements for students and teachers, a mosque, a large playground – this infrastructural adequacy as well as regular co-curricular activities at the inter-college and the national level has bestowed on this college the prestige of being one of the best colleges under Dhaka Education Board. The students after graduating from BCIC College pursue their higher studies in the most celebrated academic institutions of the country or abroad, and afterwards they begin to make conspicuous contributions to the arenas they remain engaged in. Recently, BCIC College has achieved notable success in co-curricular activities. The college has won a good number of prizes in different categories in the competitions of National Education Week, EEE Day, 1st National Television Debate, Math Olympiad and Science Project including Victory Flower. From the very inception of the class activities of the 11th Grade, BCIC College implements in sequence the syllabus, event calendar and co-curricular schedules after they are formulated. These activities are initiated and implemented by Principal himself, Vice-principal, Shift-in-charges, and the subject teachers. BCIC College is committed to ensuring excellent results with the consorted and sincere efforts of all stakeholders—students, teachers and guardians. The driving forces of BCIC College are four fundamental principles which are instilled into the students’ sublime minds to allow them become complete human beings. These basic ideologies are Discipline, Education, Morality and Humanity which help them transcend all the limitations marked in human minds from the very first day of their academic journey with BCIC College. The college has been in rapid progress in all respects with the magical touch of two talented army officers who were assigned one after another to take over to make a difference. Really, BCIC College is different than before now ----- in appearance, and in service and standard!
